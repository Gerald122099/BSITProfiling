Our capstone project focuses on designing and developing an innovative Arduino-based bottle bin that is both smart and eco-friendly.

Our primary goal is to contribute to a more efficient waste management system that not only promotes sustainability but also optimizes the use of resources.

By integrating cutting-edge technology, our smart bottle bin simplifies the recycling process, ensuring that waste is properly sorted and managed. We are passionate about leveraging technology to address pressing environmental concerns, and through this project, we aim to play a role in fostering a greener, more sustainable future.
